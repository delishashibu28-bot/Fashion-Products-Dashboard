# Fashion-Products-Dashboard

An interactive operations dashboard designed to analyze pricing strategies, consumer ratings, and brand performance metrics across various fashion product categories. This project delivers insights into major brands like Nike, H&M, Zara, Adidas, and Gucci.

## Key Features & Metrics Tracked

* **KPI Scorecards:** Fast tracking of core metrics including **Total Users (1K)**, **Average Rating (2.99/5.0)**, and **Average Price ($55.79)**.
* **Price Distribution Analyses:**
    * *Price by Brand:* Breaks down average pricing trends across leading retailers (Nike, H&M, Zara, Adidas, Gucci).
    * *Price by Category:* Donut chart distribution segmenting pricing models across Women's, Men's, and Kids' fashion.
    * *Price by Color:* Visualizes consumer pricing tiers based on item colorways (White, Yellow, Blue, Green, Black, Red).
* **Rating & Sentiment Metrics:**
    * *Rating by Product Name:* Line chart breaking down customer satisfaction trends by clothing type (Sweater, Dress, Jeans, T-shirt, Shoes).
    * *Rating by Brand:* Comparative view of customer feedback stability across the 5 main brands.
* **Interactive Slicers:** Dynamic drop-down filters at the top for `Category` and `Brand` for quick data deep-dives.

---

## Key Insights Discovered

1. **Brand Pricing Consistency:** Average item pricing stays relatively balanced across key brands, with Nike leading closely followed by H&M, Zara, Adidas, and Gucci.
2. **Product Satisfaction Tiers:** Sweaters hold the highest average rating (above 3.2), with footwear (Shoes) sitting on the lower end of the tracked inventory satisfaction curve.
3. **Color-Based Valuation:** White and Yellow themed apparel items command a slightly higher average price footprint compared to standard darker or red options.
4. **Stable Consumer Ratings:** Customer sentiment remains incredibly consistent across all core brands, sitting right around the 2.99 overall average mark.
